# The Sovereignty Series — Brand Identity Guide

## 1. Series Essence

### What It Is

An 8-volume nonfiction series about healing from narcissistic abuse and building personal sovereignty. The series follows a transformational arc from recognition through embodiment to transcendence.

**Author:** Jennifer Brooke Lawless
**Publisher:** LightField Institute
**Trim Size:** 6" x 9" paperback / Kindle ebook / Gumroad digital
**Volumes:** 8

### The Promise

You were never broken. You were conditioned. This series gives you the language to name what happened, the somatic tools to heal what your body remembers, and the framework to build a sovereign life — not as recovery, but as arrival.

### The Arc

The 8 volumes trace a single journey:

```
CRISIS → HEALING → GROUNDING → EMBODIMENT → LEGACY → SERVICE → ABUNDANCE → INTEGRATION
```

Each volume is one imperative command from the reader's truest self.

## 2. The Mantra — Titles as Brand

The single-word titles are the brand's most powerful asset. Read in sequence, they form a mantra:

### SEE → HEAL → STAND → LIVE → GIVE → SERVE → THRIVE → BECOME

This is not just a list of book titles. It is a single imperative command sequence — a map of the full healing journey. Every piece of branding, marketing, and design should treat these words as sacred. They are the brand.

## 3. The Poem — Subtitles as Through-Line

The subtitles, read together, form a single continuous poem:

> *The truth that was hidden in plain sight...*
> *The body that remembers the way home...*
> *The ground that was always yours...*
> *The presence that changes everything...*
> *The chain that ends with you...*
> *The light that doesn't consume...*
> *The life you were told you couldn't have...*
> *The self that was never lost.*

Each subtitle follows the structure: **"The [noun] that [powerful clause]"**

### The Noun Progression

The subtitle nouns trace the healing journey in miniature:

```
Truth → Body → Ground → Presence → Chain → Light → Life → Self
```

From **external recognition** (truth) through **somatic healing** (body) to **reclaimed territory** (ground) to **embodied identity** (presence) through **generational break** (chain) to **sustainable purpose** (light) to **material rebuilding** (life) to **total integration** (self).

## 5. Visual Identity

### 5A. Series Accent Color

Within the LightField Institute palette, The Sovereignty Series owns **orange-red** (`#E0522D`).

This color represents fire, truth, the courage to see and stand. It is used for:
- CTA buttons on Sovereignty-related pages
- Series hero banner accent wash
- Arc word accent coloring on dark backgrounds
- Social media cards and promotional materials
- The subtle gradient wash: `linear-gradient(135deg, #E0522D12, #FB954312)`

### 5C. Typography Hierarchy

Every cover follows the same text hierarchy:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                         │
│    The Sovereignty Series               │  ← Series name (smallest, consistent)
│    Volume N                             │  ← Volume marker (smallest, consistent)
│                                         │
│                                         │
│    SEE                                  │  ← TITLE (largest, ALL CAPS, single word)
│                                         │
│    The Truth That Was Hidden            │  ← Subtitle (medium, italic or light weight)
│    in Plain Sight                       │
│                                         │
│                                         │
│    Jennifer Brooke Lawless              │  ← Author (small but legible, consider ALL CAPS)
│                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Typography rules:**
1. **Title** — Largest, most prominent. ALL CAPS. Single word — maximum visual impact. This is the brand.
2. **Subtitle** — Medium size, italic or lighter weight. Mixed case. "The [noun] that [clause]" structure.
3. **Series/Volume line** — Small, consistent across all 8 covers. Always in the same position.
4. **Author name** — Smallest but clearly legible. Consider ALL CAPS.

**Font requirements:**
- Same font family for titles across all 8 volumes
- Same font family for subtitles across all 8 volumes
- Consistent layout structure (title position, author placement, series line position)
- Title must be readable at 80px height (thumbnail browsing on mobile)

### 5D. Cover Design Principles

- **Simple over busy** — the single-word titles are the brand; let them breathe
- **Readable at thumbnail** — most book browsing happens on mobile
- **Genre-appropriate** — self-help / personal transformation, not clinical psychology
- **No stock photos with people** — tends to look cheap at this genre level
- **No borders or frames** — can look dated
- **Dark to light arc** — the covers should visually tell the transformation story

### 5E. Spine Design

Standard spine format for each volume:

```
SEE  |  The Truth That Was Hidden in Plain Sight  |  Jennifer Brooke Lawless
```

When all 8 spines are shelved together, the titles read:

```
SEE | HEAL | STAND | LIVE | GIVE | SERVE | THRIVE | BECOME
```

**Spine rules:**
- Text reads top-to-bottom (standard US convention)
- Minimum 0.0625" clearance from spine edges
- Title is the most prominent element on the spine
- Author name at the bottom
- The 8 spines must look unified when shelved together

### 5F. Back Cover Layout

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│    The Sovereignty Series — Volume N    │  ← Series branding (top)
│    ─────────────────────────────────    │
│    [Back Cover Blurb — 100-150 words]   │  ← Sales copy
│    ─────────────────────────────────    │
│    "Tagline quote."                     │  ← Volume tagline (italic)
│    ─────────────────────────────────    │
│    [Author Photo]  [Author Bio]         │  ← Photo + short bio (50-75 words)
│    ─────────────────────────────────    │
│                          ┌──────────┐   │
│                          │ BARCODE  │   │  ← Lower-right (2" x 1.2" white space)
│                          └──────────┘   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## 6. Series Cohesion Requirements

These elements must be **identical** across all 8 volumes:

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| Title font family | Same across all 8 covers |
| Subtitle font family | Same across all 8 covers |

| Author photo | Same headshot on all 8 back covers |

| Element | Variation |
|---------|-----------|
| Color palette | Per the dark-to-light progression |
| Back cover blurb | Unique to each volume |
| Tagline | Unique to each volume |
| Imagery/texture | If used, evolves with the arc |

## 9. Marketing Identity

### Series Taglines

1. *"See. Heal. Stand. Live. Give. Serve. Thrive. Become."* — The mantra (primary)
2. *"From survival to sovereignty — one truth at a time."*
3. *"You were adapting. Now you are choosing."*
4. *"Eight words. Eight books. One unveiling."*

### Audience Statement

For emotionally sensitive adults who have survived narcissistic abuse, codependency, or enmeshed family systems — and who are ready to move from naming the patterns to building a sovereign life.

### Positioning

The Sovereignty Series sits at the intersection of:
- **Clinical psychology** (attachment theory, trauma bonding, family systems)
- **Somatic healing** (nervous system regulation, body-based therapy)
- **Practical empowerment** (scripts, decoder cards, 12-week programs)

It is NOT:
- Academic psychology (no jargon walls)
- Memoir (though informed by lived experience)
- Spiritual bypassing ("just forgive and move on")
- Revenge literature ("how to destroy your narcissist")

### Social Media Identity

| Platform | Content Type | Visual Treatment |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Instagram** | Quote cards, decoder card previews, arc word moments | 1080x1080 on cream or indigo background, arc words large |
| **Substack** | Long-form essays, free chapter excerpts, behind-the-scenes | Series header image, text-forward |
| **Gumroad** | Product listings, bundle promotions | 1600x900 product images, 3D mockups |

### Bundle Strategy

| **Vol 1 + 2** | SEE + HEAL | "Name it. Heal it." |
