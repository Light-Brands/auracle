# Personal Brand Strategy

## The Three-Layer Architecture

This document is the source of truth for the layered brand identity behind Auracle. It governs how names, voice, and presence are deployed across every surface — books, website, social, and internal systems.

---

## Positioning Statement

> **Jae Lawless is the voice behind Auracle, a system created by Jennifer Brooke (Saltzman) Lawless.**

Each layer does a different job:

- **Trust** → Jennifer Brooke (Saltzman) Lawless, M.S.
- **Connection** → Jae Lawless
- **Transformation** → Auracle

---

## Layer 1: Trust — Jennifer Brooke (Saltzman) Lawless, M.S.

**Signal:** credentialed, clinical, grounded, whole

**Why it exists:** Readers in crisis — those recognizing narcissistic patterns, recovering from trauma bonds, rebuilding attachment — need to trust the author. Clinical credentials, legal name, lived experience. This is the layer that says *you are safe here*.

**Includes:**
- Boston University, B.S. Psychology
- Nova Southeastern University, M.S. Mental Health Counseling
- Clinical experience: psychiatric units, family systems therapy, couples counseling
- The Saltzman name honors maternal lineage — "I am whole"

**Used for:**
- Book covers and title pages (no Saltzman — clean and scannable)
- Back-matter author bios (narrative credibility)
- Website About page (full identity, Saltzman included)
- Medium/long public bios (depth contexts)
- Legal, business, and internal documents

---

## Layer 2: Connection — Jae Lawless

**Signal:** modern, sharp, memorable, slightly mysterious, digital-native

**Why it exists:** This is who people feel and follow. Jae is the voice of the content, the presence on the feed, the name that carries the brand forward through culture. It has mythic undertone (Jael) without losing intimacy (Jae).

**Used for:**
- Instagram (@jae.lawless)
- Website voice and tone
- Content and copy
- Messaging and DMs
- Podcasts and interviews (front-facing persona)
- Domain: **jaelawless.com**

---

## Layer 3: Transformation — Auracle

**Signal:** elevated, intuitive + structured, spiritual-tech hybrid

**Why it exists:** Auracle is the platform — the system people enter to experience transformation. It is larger than any single person. It holds the books, the sessions, the courses, the community.

**Used for:**
- Platform name across all products
- Brand architecture for product lines
- The container that holds everything

**Products inside Auracle:**
- Private Sessions (aura readings, couples, business, group, chakra journeys)
- Group Classes
- Membership
- Sovereignty Series (8 books on recovery, healing, sovereignty)
- Energy Series (8 books on the energetic body — forthcoming)

## Domain Strategy

**Primary:** **jaelawless.com**

**Why Jae-forward:**
- Matches @jae.lawless on Instagram — zero friction between social and site
- Personal brands outlast platform names; Auracle could evolve, Jae won't
- Carries the mythic undertone (Jael) while staying approachable
- Auracle lives *inside* the site as the platform layer

**Site structure:** The site *is* Auracle in feel and function. Jae is the voice. Jennifer is the depth layer on the About page. Books, sessions, courses all live under this one roof.

---

## Relationship to Light Brands AI

Jennifer/Jae serves as **COO at Light Brands AI**, a separate venture that operates in parallel to Auracle. This is a professional identity that belongs in public bios — it signals the tech-spiritual hybrid that makes this brand distinct. Light Brands AI is not absorbed into Auracle; they are cross-linked at the person level.

**In bios:** Include "COO at Light Brands AI" alongside authorship and Auracle references.

## Voice Principles

**Jae voice:**
- Direct without being harsh
- Precise without being clinical
- Warm without being saccharine
- Mysterious without being evasive
- Modern without chasing trends

**When to speak in Jennifer's voice:** When credentials matter. When clinical precision matters. When a reader needs to know a licensed professional is behind the words.

**When to speak in Jae's voice:** Everywhere else.

2. **"I am current."** — Jae, digital-native, alive in the now
3. **"I am building something larger than me."** — Auracle, the system, the platform

## Governing Principle

When in doubt, ask: *which layer is this moment asking for?*

- Is the reader seeking safety? → **Jennifer**
- Is the reader seeking connection? → **Jae**
- Is the reader seeking transformation? → **Auracle**

Each layer is real. Each layer is true. They are not personas masking each other — they are facets of a single integrated identity, deployed with intention.
