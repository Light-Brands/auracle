## Jennifer Brooke Lawless

</div>

---

Jennifer Brooke Lawless holds a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Boston University and a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling from Nova Southeastern University. Her clinical work ranged from locked psychiatric units to family therapy, individual sessions, and couples counseling.

She wrote *Serve* for the therapists, coaches, healers, and friends-who-became-guides who gave from the wound before they had healed it. She was one of them.

For years, she mistook exhaustion for devotion and depletion for depth. She absorbed what was not hers to carry and called it empathy. *Serve* is what she learned when she finally let her boundaries become her offering — and discovered that the light which heals does not have to burn.

This book is for anyone whose healing has become their credential, and who is now ready to serve without consuming themselves in the process.

She writes and creates as **Jae Lawless** — the voice behind Auracle, the platform that holds the Sovereignty Series, her sessions, and the work beyond. She lives happily, growing with her partner Daniel of 10+ years.
