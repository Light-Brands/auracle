## Jennifer Brooke Lawless

</div>

---

Jennifer Brooke Lawless holds a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Boston University and a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling from Nova Southeastern University. Her clinical work ranged from locked psychiatric units to family therapy, individual sessions, and couples counseling.

*Become* is the capstone of the Sovereignty Series — the book she could only write after living every volume that came before it. She did not arrive at a new self. She unveiled the one she had been all along.

What looked like a journey of becoming was, in the end, a homecoming. The patterns she named in *See* had hidden her; the healing of *Heal* and *Stand* cleared the field; *Live*, *Give*, *Serve*, and *Thrive* taught her to inhabit it. *Become* is what happens when none of it is a project anymore. It is just who she is.

She wrote this book for everyone who has done the work and is ready to stop asking who they are supposed to be — and simply to live, unveiled.

She writes and creates as **Jae Lawless** — the voice behind Auracle, the platform that holds the Sovereignty Series, her sessions, and the work beyond. She lives happily, growing with her partner Daniel of 10+ years.
