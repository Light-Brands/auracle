## Jennifer Brooke Lawless

</div>

---

Jennifer Brooke Lawless holds a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Boston University and a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling from Nova Southeastern University. Her clinical work ranged from locked psychiatric units to family therapy, individual sessions, and couples counseling.

In *See* (Volume 1), she named the patterns. In *Heal* (Volume 2), she crossed from insight to embodiment. In *Stand* (Volume 3), she learned to inhabit the ground that healing created. *Live* is what she found when she stopped performing presence and started becoming it.

She spent years leading from strategy — calibrating to rooms, earning permission, winning trust through effort. The shift to coherence did not come from more discipline. It came from letting her body arrive before her words did.

She wrote *Live* for everyone who has done the healing and is now ready for the quieter question: *What does my presence feel like when it belongs to me?*

She writes and creates as **Jae Lawless** — the voice behind Auracle, the platform that holds the Sovereignty Series, her sessions, and the work beyond. She lives happily, growing with her partner Daniel of 10+ years.
