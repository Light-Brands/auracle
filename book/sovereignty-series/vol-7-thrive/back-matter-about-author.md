## Jennifer Brooke Lawless

</div>

---

Jennifer Brooke Lawless holds a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Boston University and a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling from Nova Southeastern University. Her clinical work ranged from locked psychiatric units to family therapy, individual sessions, and couples counseling.

She wrote *Thrive* because survival has a ceiling — and she hit it. After years of rebuilding her finances, her career, and her sense of what she was allowed to want, she discovered that prosperity was not a betrayal of her healing. It was the fruit of it.

*Thrive* is for everyone who learned to make themselves small for safety and is now relearning the vocabulary of abundance, ambition, and joy. Not as rebellion, but as the natural expansion of a sovereign life.

You were told that wanting more was proof you had not healed. This book is the counter-evidence.

She writes and creates as **Jae Lawless** — the voice behind Auracle, the platform that holds the Sovereignty Series, her sessions, and the work beyond. She lives happily, growing with her partner Daniel of 10+ years.
