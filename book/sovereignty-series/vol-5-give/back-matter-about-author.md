## Jennifer Brooke Lawless

</div>

---

Jennifer Brooke Lawless holds a Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Boston University and a Master of Science in Mental Health Counseling from Nova Southeastern University. Her clinical work ranged from locked psychiatric units to family therapy, individual sessions, and couples counseling.

She wrote *Give* because the question eventually arrives for everyone who does this work: *What do I refuse to pass on?*

In *See*, she named the patterns she was raised inside. In *Heal* and *Stand*, she did the repair and the reclaiming. *Give* is about the chain — and about learning that breaking it is not one grand act but a thousand small choices to repair instead of repeat, to attune instead of react, to trust a child's "no" more than your own reflex to control.

She wrote this book for every parent, every future parent, and every person carrying someone else's inheritance in their body, who is ready to become the break in the pattern rather than its continuation.

She writes and creates as **Jae Lawless** — the voice behind Auracle, the platform that holds the Sovereignty Series, her sessions, and the work beyond. She lives happily, growing with her partner Daniel of 10+ years.
