### Part I — Recognition

**Aligned with Chapters 1–3**

#### 1. The Shrinking Map
- *Where do you feel smaller?* — Guided inventory of current relationships
- Rate each relationship: Do you feel larger or smaller after contact?
- Body sensation log: What does "smaller" feel like in your chest, throat, shoulders?

#### 2. Your Body Already Knows
- 7-day body awareness journal (simple daily check-in, 2 minutes)
- Sensation vocabulary builder — naming what you feel beyond "fine" and "stressed"
- The "After" exercise: Track how you feel after each significant interaction for one week

#### 3. Pattern Naming
- Personal decoder worksheet: Match your experiences to the 60 patterns
- "The one that made my breath catch" — Deep dive on your top 3 recognized patterns
- Timeline mapping: When did each pattern first appear in your life?

---

### Part II — Understanding

**Aligned with Chapters 4–6**

#### 4. The Bond That Felt Like Love
- Slot machine vs. vending machine: Map the unpredictability in your relationship
- The cycle tracker: Identify your tension → incident → reconciliation → calm loop
- Relief vs. love: Journal prompts for distinguishing the two
- The 72-Hour Test log (template for 4 weeks of use)

#### 5. Seeing Behind the Mask
- The gap exercise: Who they present vs. who they are (side-by-side inventory)
- Injury mapping: What are the "small" criticisms that triggered disproportionate rage?
- Role identification: Which of the 12 masks does your person wear most? What does it cost you?

---

### Part III — Origins

**Aligned with Chapters 7–10**

#### 6. The Role You Were Assigned
- Family role identification: Golden Child, Scapegoat, Lost Child, or Mascot
- "The rules I learned" — List the unspoken rules of your family system
- Who decided who you were allowed to love?

#### 7. The Sentence You Spent Your Childhood Earning
- Guided freewrite: What did your parent never say that you kept trying to earn?
- Mother/father wound identification (from Chapter 8 archetypes)
- Body letter: Let your body write one paragraph about your childhood

#### 8. Your Template
- Childhood pattern inventory: What did love require from you?
- Connect the line: How your childhood template shows up in adult relationships
- The "too sensitive" reclamation: List every accurate perception that was dismissed

#### 9. Siblings & the System
- Sibling relationship map: Regulated vs. dysregulated exchanges
- The alliance that never formed: What would you say to your sibling if the system wasn't listening?
- Scripts practice: Write your own version of the boundary scripts from Chapter 10

---

### Part IV — Application

**Aligned with Chapters 11–14**

#### 10. The Relationship Timeline
- Map the three phases: When was idealization? Devaluation? Discard/cycling?
- "What I called it then vs. what I call it now" — Renaming key moments
- Red flag retrospective: What you see now that you couldn't see then

> **A Note on Co-Parenting** — Sidebar for readers sharing children with a narcissist

#### 11. The Hidden Costs
- Beyond romance: Inventory manipulation in friendships, work, spiritual spaces, family
- "The relationship nobody suspects" — Guided reflection on the one that looks fine but costs the most
- Energy audit: Where is your life force going?

#### 12. Your Exposure Questions
- Safety assessment (GREEN / YELLOW / RED) before proceeding
- Select 3 exposure questions most relevant to your situation
- Response prediction: What do you expect them to say? What would a healthy response look like?
- Journal: Which question are you most afraid to ask, and what does that fear tell you?

#### 13. Your Emergency Protocol Card
- Tear-out / printable emergency protocol (the 6 steps)
- Personalized grounding menu: Which techniques work best for YOUR body?
- "Whose needs are being centered?" — Wallet-sized reminder card
- Body decoder cheat sheet: Chest tight = pressured, confusion = reality distortion, etc.

---

### Part V — Recovery & Rewiring

**Aligned with Chapters 15–17**

#### 14. Energetic Remapping Journal
- Loop recognition log: When the old pattern activates, what happens in your body?
- "This belongs to then, not now" — Practice translating body sensations to pattern language
- 3 truths about me (regardless of anyone's evaluation): Write them. Say them. Repeat them.
- Shame inventory: What shame are you carrying that was never yours?

#### 15. Your Scripts & Responses
- Spiritual aikido practice: Rewrite your most common arguments as redirections
- The dreaded conversation planner: Prepare for a specific upcoming interaction
- Exit line library: Write 5 sentences that end conversations on your terms
- "What I wish I could say" — The uncensored version (just for you, never to send)
- **When you want to go back** — The exercise for the worst night (72-hour protocol, naming the pull, calling your safe person)

#### 16. Moving Forward
- The grief inventory: What you're mourning that you never actually had
- Contradictory emotions map: What you feel simultaneously (relief + grief, anger + gratitude)
- "I believe myself" — Somatic exercise with guided reflection
- The sovereignty statement: Write your own version of the closing declaration
- Letter to yourself one year from now
