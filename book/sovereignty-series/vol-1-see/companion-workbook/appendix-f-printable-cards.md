> Cut these out. Photograph them. Tape them to your mirror.
> Keep them where you can reach them when you can't think clearly.

```

│                                             │

│                                             │
```

> **How to use these cards:**
>
> - **Print this page** and cut along the borders
> - **Photograph each card** and save to a locked folder on your phone
> - **Tape your most-needed card** to your bathroom mirror, car dashboard, or inside a notebook
> - **Keep the Emergency Protocol and Grounding cards** where you can access them without thinking
> - **Give the Universal Response Bank** to a trusted friend so they can remind you
>
> When you're activated, you won't be able to think of these.
> That's why they need to be where your eyes can find them
> when your brain can't.
