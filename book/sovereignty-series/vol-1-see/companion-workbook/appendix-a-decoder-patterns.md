# Appendix A: Quick Reference — All 60 Decoder Patterns

> One-page summary of each pattern with the power move.
> Keep this reference nearby. When something confusing happens, scan the list.
> Naming breaks the spell.

---

## How to Use This Reference

When you feel confused, activated, or "off" after an interaction:

1. Scan this list
2. Find the pattern that matches
3. Read the power move
4. Breathe

You don't need to identify the exact card. A rough match is enough. The act of naming is what breaks the loop.

---

## Card 0: The Wolf in Sheep's Clothing (Master Card)

**The Pattern:** Every manipulation tactic shares one thing — control disguised as something virtuous (care, honesty, protection, love).

**The Test:** After this interaction, do I feel more myself or less myself?

**Power Move:** Stop looking at the clothing. Start noticing the impact.

## The One Question That Covers Everything

When you can't identify the specific pattern, return to Card 0:

> *After this interaction, do I feel more myself or less myself?*

Genuine care makes you larger. Control makes you smaller.

That's the only decoder you need.
