# Vol 1: SEE — Website Appendixes & Resources

> **Decision:** All appendixes and resources for Vol 1 live on the website — **not** in the printed book. The book references the website exactly once per relevant chapter. The website does the directory work; the book reads like literature and doesn't age.

This document is the master source for everything that moves from the printed manuscript to the companion resource page at the author's domain.

---

## Why Everything Goes Online

**The concern:** Phone numbers change. Hotlines reorganize. URLs break. Apps get discontinued. Reddit communities get banned. A book filled with specific crisis numbers and resource links will date itself. The author wants this book to be **canon** — to live in the literature the way *The Body Keeps the Score* or *Why Does He Do That?* lives. Those books don't have appendices full of 1-800 numbers.

**The reality of the current manuscript:**

- Appendix A is 607 lines of phone numbers, hotline listings, app recommendations, Reddit communities, YouTube channels, and specific URLs
- At least 15 places in the main chapters link to Appendix A for crisis resources
- The deprecated chapters contain even more embedded phone numbers (1-800-799-7233, 988, etc.)
- The resource will begin degrading the moment the book is published

**The decision: Everything appendix-like and resource-like moves to the website.**

The printed book contains only one repeated crisis statement, wherever needed:

> *"If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services or a domestic violence hotline in your country."*

No specific numbers. No specific organizations. This sentence will still be true in fifty years.

Every instance of "See Appendix A for crisis resources" in the main chapters is replaced with one of two options:

1. The timeless statement above.
2. A single companion URL reference (used sparingly, probably once per relevant chapter at most), in a format like:

> **For current crisis hotlines, support communities, apps, legal resources, and financial assistance programs — including resources specific to LGBTQ+, immigrant, disabled, and culturally diverse survivors — visit [companion URL].**
>
> *This online resource is maintained and updated regularly so the information you find there is current, even if this book was published years ago.*

**The result:**

- **Longevity** — nothing in the print edition can go stale
- **Expandability** — the online resource can grow without new print editions
- **Cultural specificity** — the online resource can include region-specific, language-specific, and community-specific resources that would bloat the book
- **The author's continued presence** — readers who visit the page encounter the author's current work, community, and offerings. The book becomes a gateway, not a dead end.

## What Stays in the Printed Book

Nothing that dates. A small number of timeless, author-voice pieces may remain as short in-chapter passages (not as a formal appendix):

- The author's **Safety Planning** reflection and checklist — the personal note ("I did this too"), the checklist, the code words strategy. Timeless lived wisdom, not a phone directory.
- The **questions to ask potential therapists** and the **red flags in therapists** list — timeless guidance.
- The **90-Day Forward Protocol** — the author's original framework.
- The **"For Helpers and Supporters"** passage — timeless advice.
- The **Signs of Healing** list — timeless.

Even these pieces are woven into the chapter flow rather than sectioned off as an appendix. There is no "Appendix A" in the printed book.

**Everything else — every phone number, every hotline, every app, every Reddit community, every URL, every organization name — lives online.**

## Implementation Notes for the Manuscript

1. Remove the standalone "Appendix A: Resources" section from the printed manuscript.
2. Replace every in-chapter "See Appendix A" pointer with either the timeless crisis statement or the single companion URL reference.
3. Ensure the companion URL appears at most once per relevant chapter — not every page.
4. Host and maintain the companion resource page at the author's domain; assign an owner for quarterly review.
5. Include in the book's front matter a short line introducing the companion resource and the reason it lives online (longevity, accuracy, expandability).
