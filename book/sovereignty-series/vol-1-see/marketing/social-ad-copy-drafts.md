# Social Ad Copy Drafts — Instagram & Facebook

Working drafts of paid social copy for Vol 1: *See*. Modeled on the
short-line, single-statement-per-frame format that performs well in the
"healing your inner child / childhood trauma test" ad category (e.g., Liven,
BetterHelp, Mended Light).

Each ad below is structured as a sequence of frames. One sentence per frame.
Use over slow B-roll or a single steady talking-head shot. Caption text should
match the on-screen text exactly so the ad reads with sound off.

Headline copy is in the series' voice: direct, second-person, no hype, no
clinical jargon unless the term itself is the hook.

## Production notes

- **Voice:** second-person, declarative, no questions in the first three
  frames. Save questions for the CTA card.
- **Pacing:** one statement per frame. Resist combining lines. The white
  space is the persuasion.
- **Imagery:** the competitor ads use AI-generated jungle/wilderness footage.
  Differentiate with real B-roll — hands writing in a notebook, a window at
  dusk, a doorway, an unmade bed. Quiet, domestic, recognizable.
- **Avoid:** the word "narcissist" in frame 1 of cold-audience ads. It
  pre-filters. Lead with the *experience*; name the pattern by frame 4 or 5.
- **Compliance:** no diagnostic claims, no "cure," no before/after
  testimonials with specific outcomes. Phrases like "name what you've been
  living" and "see the pattern" are safe.
