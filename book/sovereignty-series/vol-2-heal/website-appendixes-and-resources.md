# Vol 2: HEAL — Website Appendixes & Resources

> **Decision (carried forward from Vol 1):** All appendixes and resources for the Sovereignty Series live on the companion website — **not** in the printed book. The book references the website sparingly. The website does the directory work; the book reads like literature and doesn't age.

This document is the master source for everything that moves from the Vol 2 printed manuscript to the companion resource page at the author's domain. It mirrors the structure established in `../vol-1-see/website-appendixes-and-resources.md` and extends it with the recovery-phase material specific to HEAL.

---

## Why Everything Goes Online (Recap)

Phone numbers change. Hotlines reorganize. URLs break. Apps get discontinued. A book filled with specific crisis numbers and resource links dates itself. The Sovereignty Series is meant to live in the literature the way *The Body Keeps the Score* or *Why Does He Do That?* lives — canon, not a directory.

The printed book contains only one repeated crisis statement, wherever needed:

> *"If you are in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services or a domestic violence hotline in your country."*

Every pointer to the companion website uses a format like:

> **For current crisis hotlines, culturally-grounded therapist directories, community-specific support organizations, and continued-learning resources, visit [companion URL].**
>
> *This online resource is maintained and updated regularly so the information you find there is current, even if this book was published years ago.*

The companion site does the living work: region-specific crisis resources, language-specific hotlines, per-community organizations, updated reading lists, and the author's continued presence (courses, community, writing).
