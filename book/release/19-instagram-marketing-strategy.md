## 7. Engagement Strategy

### Community Building

- Reply to every comment within 2 hours during the first 30 days (strong algorithmic signal)
- Use engagement prompts on every post:
  - "Which one sounds familiar?"
  - "Save this for when you need it"
  - "Tag someone who needs to see this"
  - "What's your go-to power move?"
- Pin top 3 comments on each post (preferably personal stories — social proof)
- Match the brand voice: empathetic, precise, embodied, empowering, direct

### Hashtag Strategy

Use 8–10 hashtags per post, rotated across three tiers:

| Tier | Size | Examples | Per Post |
|------|------|---------|----------|
| Large (500K–5M posts) | Discovery | #narcissisticabuse #traumahealing #toxicrelationships #emotionalabuse #healingjourney | 3–4 |
| Medium (50K–500K) | Niche | #narcissisticabuserecovery #secureattachment #nervoussystemhealing #traumabond #codependencyrecovery | 3–4 |
| Small / branded | Community | #thesovereigntyseries #sovereigntyjourney | 2–3 |

**Branded hashtag:** `#TheSovereigntySeries` — use on every post, encourage readers to use when sharing their own content.

Full hashtag sets by theme are in `10-social-media-templates.md`.

### Collaboration Targets

- Trauma-informed therapists on Instagram (shared audience, credibility exchange)
- Bookstagram reviewers in the self-help/psychology niche
- Podcast hosts in the narcissistic abuse recovery space (repurpose interviews to Reels)
- Other authors in adjacent niches (joint Lives, shared carousels)

## 8. Growth Strategy (0 → 5,000 Followers)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1–4) — Target: 500 Followers

- Publish 20+ posts before launch to create a grid worth following
- Post 5x/week, heavy on Reels for discovery reach
- Cross-promote: every Substack newsletter includes Instagram handle + "this week on Instagram" section
- Every 3rd post CTAs to free Decoder Cards (the email capture flywheel)
- Jennifer personally engages in 5–10 relevant accounts' comment sections daily (genuine comments, not spam)
- Seed initial followers from existing Substack list, personal network, and @jae.lawless cross-promotion

### Phase 2: Traction (Weeks 5–12) — Target: 2,000 Followers

- Double down on the Reel formats that got the most views in Phase 1
- Collaborate with 2–3 therapist accounts for carousel swaps or joint Lives
- Consider boosting the top-performing Reel ($5–10/day for 7 days)
- "Follow on Instagram" in every Gumroad delivery email and Substack welcome sequence
- Volume 2 (HEAL) launch creates a natural content peak

### Phase 3: Momentum (Weeks 13–26) — Target: 5,000 Followers

- User-generated content from readers, testimonial Reels, "reader spotlight" Stories
- Monthly Decoder Cards giveaway (follow + tag a friend to enter)
- Explore Instagram broadcast channel for direct subscriber communication
- Volumes 3–5 launches provide ongoing content peaks

### Reels Growth Specifics

- Post Reels between 7–9 AM or 6–8 PM Costa Rica time (UTC-6) — test both slots
- Front-load the most powerful sentence in the first 1.5 seconds
- Use 3–5 relevant hashtags on Reels (fewer than feed posts)
- Share every Reel to Stories immediately after posting
- Check the Reels audio library weekly for trending sounds that match the emotional tone (contemplative, empowering)
- The hooks from `10-social-media-templates.md` are already optimized for short-form video

## 9. Metrics & KPIs

### Weekly Tracking

| Metric | Weeks 1–4 | Weeks 5–12 | Weeks 13–26 |
|--------|-----------|------------|-------------|
| Followers | 500 | 2,000 | 5,000 |
| Reach per post | 500–1,000 | 1,000–5,000 | 5,000–15,000 |
| Saves per post | 10+ | 25+ | 50+ |
| Shares per post | 5+ | 15+ | 30+ |
| Bio link clicks | 20/week | 50/week | 100/week |
| Decoder Card downloads | 10/week | 25/week | 50/week |
| Reels avg views | 200+ | 1,000+ | 3,000+ |
| Engagement rate | 5–8% | 5–8% | 5–8% |

### Monthly Review Questions

1. Which post got the most **saves**? → Create more of that format/topic
2. Which post got the most **shares**? → Replicate the hook and format
3. Which Reel had the highest **completion rate**? → Optimize hook and length
4. What questions are people asking in **comments and DMs**? → Content goldmine
5. Which topic drove the most **link-in-bio clicks**? → Conversion signal
6. Did any post drive measurable **book sales or Decoder Card downloads**?

### Benchmarks (Under 5K Followers)

- Good engagement rate: 5–8% (likes + comments + saves ÷ followers)
- Good Reel view rate: 2–5x follower count
- Good save rate: 2–5% of reach
- Good share rate: 1–3% of reach

### Tools

- Instagram Insights (native analytics — requires business/creator account)
- Linktree analytics for click tracking
- Gumroad analytics for conversion tracking from Instagram
- Simple spreadsheet tracking the weekly metrics above
