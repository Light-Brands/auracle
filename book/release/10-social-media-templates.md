### Post 3: The Disguises

**Hook:** Control never announces itself.

**Content:**
Control doesn't walk into the room with a sign.

It arrives dressed as:
→ Concern: "I was just checking on you."
→ Love: "I only act this way because I care."
→ Honesty: "Someone needs to tell you the truth."
→ Protection: "I'm trying to keep you safe."
→ Logic: "You're being emotional."

Each sounds reasonable.
That's what makes them so effective.

### Post 4: The 3-3-3 Rule

**Hook:** When activated, try this.

**Content:**
The 3-3-3 Rule for responding in the moment:

**3 Seconds:** Pause. Count silently. Interrupt the automatic reaction.

**3 Breaths:** Inhale 4, exhale 6. Engage your calm nervous system.

**3 Words (or fewer):** "I'll think about that." "Interesting perspective." "Not for me."

The less you say, the less they have to twist.

**CTA:** Save this. You'll need it.
