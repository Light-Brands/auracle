## Design Notes

### Title Pattern

- All 8 volumes use a **single imperative word** as the title
- Each title is a command from the reader's truest self
- Titles form a mantra when read in sequence: *See. Heal. Stand. Live. Give. Serve. Thrive. Become.*

### Subtitle Pattern

- Each subtitle follows the structure: **"The [noun] that [powerful clause]"**
- Read together, they form a single continuous poem
- The subtitles provide the emotional context; the titles provide the directive

### Typography Hierarchy

1. **TITLE** — Largest, most prominent. ALL CAPS. White. Single word — maximum visual impact.
2. **Subtitle** — Medium size, italic or lighter weight. Off-white / warm white. Mixed case. "The [noun] that [clause]" structure.
3. **Series/Volume line** — Small, off-white / warm white, consistent across all 8 covers.
4. **Author name** — Smallest but clearly legible. Off-white / warm white. Consider ALL CAPS.
5. **Accent details** — Subtle gold. Thin rules, dividers, or small decorative marks that add warmth and premium feel.

### Spine Text (Paperback)

```
SEE  |  Recognizing Narcissistic Manipulation in Relationships, Family, and Work  |  Jennifer Brooke Lawless, M.S.
```

When all 8 spines are lined up, the titles read:

```
SEE | HEAL | STAND | LIVE | GIVE | SERVE | THRIVE | BECOME
```

### Color Progression Suggestion

The series should move from darker tones (recognition, crisis) to lighter/warmer tones (healing, embodiment) to radiant tones (sovereignty, transcendence):

| Vol | Title | Background Color Direction |
|:---:|:-----:|---------------------------|
| 1 | SEE | Deep blue — clarity, revelation |
| 2 | HEAL | Warm earthy green — growth, grounding |
| 3 | STAND | Designer's choice — steady, centered |
| 4 | LIVE | Dark pink — vitality, embodiment |
| 5 | GIVE | Warm brown — nurturing, earth |
| 6 | SERVE | Soft green — renewal, purpose |
| 7 | THRIVE | Rich green — abundance, expansion |
| 8 | BECOME | White — transcendence, arrival |

**Title and subtitle text:** White across all 8 volumes for maximum contrast and series cohesion.

### Series Cohesion Requirements

- Same font family across all 8 covers
- **All 8 titles must be the same size** — equal visual weight regardless of character count
- White titles and subtitles across all volumes
- Identical layout structure (title position, author placement)
- "The Sovereignty Series" subtitle on each
- Volume number in consistent position
- Single-word title dominates the cover — this is the brand

## Companion Workbook Covers

Each volume has an accompanying **companion workbook**. The workbook covers should be **identical to the main volume covers** — same background color, same white title and subtitle, same layout — with a "Companion Workbook" designation added below the subtitle.

The workbook designation should be:
- White text, lighter weight or smaller size than the subtitle
- Consider decorative rules above and below: `── Companion Workbook ──`
- Clear enough to distinguish from the main volume, subtle enough to still read as part of the set
- When the main volume and its workbook are shelved side by side, they should look like a matched pair

### Workbook Spine Text

```
SEE  |  Companion Workbook  |  Jennifer Brooke Lawless, M.S.
```
