## Bundle Overview

This comprehensive course bundle transforms the 8-volume Sovereignty Series into an immersive, AI-narrated learning experience. Each course stands alone as a complete transformation, yet together they create a progressive journey from recognition through embodiment and beyond.

**Total Learning Hours:** ~80 hours across all 8 courses
**Format:** Pre-recorded AI-narrated video lessons with exercises
**Delivery:** Self-paced with lifetime access

## The Eight Courses

### Course 1. See
**Stage:** SEE | **Duration:** ~10 hours | **Lessons:** 24
> *"This wasn't love—this was conditioning."*

Learn to recognize and name manipulation patterns. Decode 60 patterns of control in real-time. Reclaim your perception.

**Ideal For:** Anyone who has felt confused, gaslit, or drained by relationships

### Course 2. Heal
**Stage:** HEAL | **Duration:** ~12 hours | **Lessons:** 28
> *"Leaving hurts because your body learned danger as connection."*

Retrain your nervous system for secure attachment. Understand why insight alone isn't enough. Build the bridge from trauma to trust.

**Ideal For:** Those ready to heal attachment wounds and date differently

### Course 3. Stand
**Stage:** STAND | **Duration:** ~10 hours | **Lessons:** 23
> *"You are safe to choose without urgency."*

Install internal authority. Own your emotional field. Lead from presence rather than performance.

**Ideal For:** Those ready to stop people-pleasing and start living from their own center

### Course 4. Live
**Stage:** LIVE | **Duration:** ~9 hours | **Lessons:** 22
> *"Your presence is erotic when it belongs to you."*

Inhabit your body fully. Master energy hygiene. Lead with magnetism that doesn't manipulate.

**Ideal For:** Those ready to embody their power in relationships and leadership

---

### Course 5. Give
**Stage:** GIVE | **Duration:** ~11 hours | **Lessons:** 24
> *"You don't have to be healed to be a good parent. You have to be healing."*

Break generational cycles. Parent through your triggers. Raise emotionally literate humans.

**Ideal For:** Parents healing while parenting, especially with difficult co-parenting situations

---

### Course 6. Serve
**Stage:** SERVE | **Duration:** ~12 hours | **Lessons:** 26
> *"Your healing is your credential. Your boundaries are your offering."*

Hold others' healing without losing your own. Navigate the ethics of lived experience. Build sustainable helping practices.

**Ideal For:** Healers, coaches, therapists, and those called to serve survivors

---

### Course 7. Thrive
**Stage:** THRIVE | **Duration:** ~11 hours | **Lessons:** 24
> *"You are allowed to thrive, not just survive."*

Rebuild financial sovereignty. Navigate workplace dynamics. Build a life that holds you.

**Ideal For:** Those ready to move from survival mode to material prosperity

---

### Course 8. Become
**Stage:** BECOME | **Duration:** ~10 hours | **Lessons:** 24
> *"You were never becoming—you were always unveiling."*

Embody your infinite self. Live from quantum consciousness. Complete the integration.

**Ideal For:** Those ready to transcend survival identity and step into full expression

## How Courses Are Structured

### Module Format
Each course is divided into 4-6 modules, with each module containing:
- Welcome/overview video
- 3-6 core lesson videos
- Integration exercises
- Module reflection

### Lesson Format
Each lesson follows a consistent structure:
1. **Opening** — Context and intention setting
2. **Core Teaching** — Main concepts with examples
3. **Practice** — Guided exercise or reflection
4. **Integration** — How to apply in daily life
5. **Bridge** — Connection to next lesson

### Video Specifications
- Length: 15-30 minutes per lesson
- Style: AI narrator with visual support
- Accessibility: Closed captions, transcript downloads
